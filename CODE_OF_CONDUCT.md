# Contributor Covenant Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge
We pledge to make participation in our project a harassment-free experience for everyone.

## Our Standards
Examples of behavior that contributes to a positive environment include:
- Using welcoming and inclusive language
- Being respectful of differing viewpoints

## Enforcement Responsibilities
Project maintainers are responsible for clarifying standards and taking corrective action.

## Scope
This Code of Conduct applies within project spaces and public spaces when representing the project.

## Enforcement
Instances of abusive behavior may be reported to project maintainers.

## Enforcement Guidelines
Consequences may range from warnings to permanent bans.
